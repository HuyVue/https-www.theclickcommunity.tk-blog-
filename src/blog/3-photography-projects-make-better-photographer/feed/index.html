Hi Steve,

This post, and blog in its entirety, isn’t geared solely towards pro/seasoned photographers. So many of the tips you find here will be applicable to different levels of expertise. Kudos to you for lasting 11 months as taking on a 365 project is no easy feat. I know pro photographers who still complete 365 projects for various reasons. It helps them constantly practice being in a creative state. It might also be that they’re working on specific techniques and shooting daily helps keep them accountable to practice. Or they might even just want to capture certain aspects (like their family) on a daily basis. Seeing it as “making” themselves do something is, in my opinion, the wrong perspective.They do it because they enjoy the exercise and because accomplishing a goal you’ve set is an awesome feeling, no matter what the goal is. Thank you for chiming in!

I do think the three-month project has its place, especially for the seasoned among you. For the rookies, however, I think a 365 project is relevant. Just remember, it isn’t a 365 project.

We are celebrating International Women's Day with the release or our much anticipated list of 100 FEMALE Photographers to Watch in 2020.

We relied on nominations from our Click Pro network to present a list of 100 women pushing the boundaries of their respective genres. This year's group is sure to inspire you while revealing something that's never been much of a secret:

The most exceptional photographers in every
genre, all over the world, across the spectrum of
age, race, and culture are also WOMEN.

Whether she's photographing clients or her own family, Aimee captures the essence of her subjects. Look through her feed and you will see some stunning self-portraits, too!

@AIMEEGLUCINAPHOTOGRAPHY

Clean, crisp, and focused defines Alex's photography regardless of subject. She photographs interiors, products and people with a clear sense of purpose and invitation to come in or to meet a new colleague.

A few of our favorite things: 2015 holiday edition

3 Comments / Products & Pretty Things / By Click Community Blog
Share137
Tweet
Pin1
138 Shares

It’s time for a long running favorite, the annual CMteam Favorite Things list! We do not shy away from our love of shopping and helping others find great new products. Plus, it’s just in time to add a few new things to your wish list for the holidays. Make life easier and just send this post to your loved ones

  1. Chemex: My husband heard that pour-over was the next big thing for coffee lovers and got me started last Christmas. I’ve been through several brewing devices — Hario, Kalita, Clever Coffee Dripper — but the Chemex is my favorite. It looks like a beautiful chemist’s tool, which is appropriate for the amount of care I put in to crafting my coffee each morning. I’ve never had such good coffee (but I will say that fresh beans, a great grinder, and precise temperature control are critical parts of the process!).
  2. PopSugar Must Have Subscription box: I have tried a lot of subscription boxes — from food to health to clothing to cosmetics — but this holds the title as my favorite. It’s like an amazing care package from a girlfriend with a bunch of her favorite things inside. There are always at least two things I love and one thing, a handful that are just fun, and one thing I end up passing along to someone else (which is actually a good thing!).
  3. BECCA Beach Tint Shimmer Souffl: This was the first Becca product I tried, and it completely converted me to the whole line. It brings an extraordinarily natural flush to the face, like you’ve just been kissed by the sun or come in from brisk walk in the snow.
  4. FRYE Campus Stitch Tote: This is a pretty and durable all-purpose tote. It doesn’t have a lot of pockets (just one, and I keep my iPhone in it), which is exactly what makes it a “throw whatever you need in here” bag — change of clothes, a laptop, my camera, notebooks, whatever.

  1. Icebreaker: I spend a lot of time outside hiking and skiing, and most of my clothing (with the exception of my jeans!) is 100% merino wool– even my warm weather clothes! Icebreaker is one of my favorite brands for foundation pieces.
  2. Profoto Reflector SunSilver: This is one of my favorite tools– I use this reflector when I want to create a pocket of light for my subject that replicates the look of sunlight. It comes in a few different sizes and has handles (which I love)– I have a few of the sizes, but the small version is my favorite because it easily fits in my pack.
  3. Justin’s Organic Peanut Butter Cups: These are UNBELIEVABLE. I don’t eat a lot of candy, but I simply cannot resist these! They aren’t as sweet are the “other” peanut butter cups, and I simply adore dark chocolate.
  4. Fujifilm X-T10: This is the camera that I carry everywhere with me! It’s small and light enough to take anywhere you go (seriously– the body is the size of two iPhones stacked together) and yet it’s full-featured enough that I can use it for some of my toughest jobs. Pair it with a small lens like the 18mm and it’s the perfect go-anywhere camera. Pair it with the 56 f/1.2 and the combinations is truly magical.
  5. Hudson Collin Skinny Jeans: I have a few pairs of jeans that I simply adore, but my Hudson Collins are my current favorites. I can pull my favorite pair of Frye boots on over them and I’m ready to go!

  1. Stella and Dot “On the Mark” necklace (gold): My six year old picked out this necklace for me a few years ago, which makes it extra special to me. I love how simple and delicate it is, and it goes with just about anything!
  2. Hydro Cool Firming Eye Gels: Anyone who is familiar with the effects of sleepless night (travel, long work hours, children who seem to always be awake?) will love these! Place them under your eyes for 15 minutes and see tired, puffy eyes disappear. I would stock pile them if I could!
  3. Breville Juice Fountain: I try to have at least one big glass of vegetable juice a day – being perpetually sleep deprived, I swear it gives me more energy than coffee! This particular juicer is fantastic because it can handle just about any kind of produce, and even after several years of use, it still works great! Best part? My son loves to help me juice and will happily “drink” kale, chard, and other veggies he might not otherwise touch!
  4. T3 Featherweight Luxe 2I Dryer: While it might sound ridiculous to spend this much on a hair dryer (and I’ll admit, it kind of is), I promise it’s the best! This hairdryer has not only lasted me a solid decade, but it cut my drying time in half! It leaves my thick, wavy hair straight and shiny. It’s worth the splurge!

  1. L’Occitane One Minute Manicure: My hands get so dry from washing them constantly. I love this exfoliating and softening sugar rub with shea butter. I use it every night before bed and it leaves my hands nice and soft.
  2. Anthropologie Monogram Mugs: I’m a bit of a mug collector and I love the unique shape of these lightweight mugs with a first initial on them. This is one of my favorite mugs for my morning cup of coffee.
  3. Lacie Mini Rugged External Hard Drives: After a couple of my EHDs died, an Apple employee recommended these to me and they have worked out so much better for me.
  4. Contigo Autoseal Pinnacle Travel Mugs: I love these travel mugs because they keep my coffee hot for hours and they have a lock to prevent accidental spills. These are a necessity for my sunrise shoots

  1. Restoration Hardware Maxwell Upholstered Sofa: When we moved about a year ago the first thing I wanted to do was make a purchase for our home that made it feel homey and cozy. A new sofa was just the right thing for our new space. I wanted comfy and big enough for all 6 of us to sit and lay on. This is the one we bought and I love it, because all of us can literally be laid out on it snuggling from end to end, it’s awesome!
  2. Color Me Calm: Remember how it felt to be a kid and just doodle and color? I still love to color and recently found some beautiful adult coloring books specifically geared towards helping us slow down, relax and de-stress. This is one of my favorites!
  3. Google Calendar: I can’t believe I ever lived without this calendar! I can barely remember where and when I am supposed to be most days or when things are due for my kids and I, so in an effort to be more prompt and efficient I loaded up this app on my phone and now, I can’t live without it! Syncs with your computer to your phone and it allows you to put reminders on your significant others (or anyone else for that matter!) calendar as well!
  4. Color Inc. Pro Lab: This is my go to print lab. They have a variety of gorgeous products, their color is amazing, fast production time, and the quality is fantastic!
  5. Lensbaby Velvet 56: A classic portrait lens that I’ve fallen in love with. It’s both portrait and macro, manual focus, and opens up to f/1.6 allowing you to craft your vision with a velvety dreamy look down to capturing the smallest detail. This is one of my favorite creative lenses to use.

  1. Hopper: Whether I am daydreaming about taking a trip or actually planning one, I love this app for researching flights. It has suggestions for finding the best deal on airfare and I love that it notifies me when it is time to buy.
  2. Emily Ley To Do Notepad: This notepad keeps me on top of my never ending to-do list. I write out the most important things I need to get done each day and I am able to stay on task. With only eight spots to fill in, my list never gets too long and stays manageable.
  3. The Wet Brush: I originally bought this for my daughter’s long hair because brushing it was torture for her (and me!). We now own a few of them because not only does my daughter not mind getting her hair brushed anymore, but I love to use it as well!
  4. Jessica Drossin Force of Nature Bundle Pack: This overlay pack is so much fun to play with in post-processing! It is perfect for adding just a little something more to your image without it being too much.
  5. Countertop Cereal Dispenser: We have 4 of these and they have been life changing in our house. They keep the cereal mess in the mornings to a minimum while still keeping everything fresh. My kids love using them too!

  1. CUTCO knives: We’ve always heard amazing things about these knives and how crazy sharp they are. So we were lucky enough to have caught a road show at our Costco and splurged for our Anniversary. Can I just say they are all that and a bag of chips. These knives are impressive and they leave scars when you slice you finger by accident when you clean them.
  2. Overdrive and Kindle: I love being able to check out books from my local library and read them on my Kindle app. I’ve been able to read so many more books this way, I always keep a few on my wait list, so that I can always have something interesting to read.
  3. Harveys Seatbelt Purse: I just bought my first Harvey’s Seatbelt messenger bag this week and I am really excited try it out. It’s super sturdy and I love the feet on the bottom. Plus they come in lots of fun and cool colors.
  4. Maui Jim Kihei Sunglasses: Unfortunately Maui Jim discontinued these glasses but they are the most comfortable and lightweight sunglasses I have ever owned. And they don’t pull my hair when I use them as a headband. Maybe Maui Jim will bring them back. But if they don’t they have a ton of other cute lightweight styles to choose from.

  1. Waze app: An app that gives directions based on real-time information reported by other app users….hands down the BEST app to get from point A to point B!!!
  2. 13″ macbook pro: Love, love, love it! Fast and light, I use it for both photo editing and my non-photo work. I bring it everywhere I go!
  3. Music by Florence and the Machine: Amazing vocals and lyrics – my boys enjoy it too!
  4. Dansko sneakers: Super comfortable and cute. They have become my designated weekend wear.
  5. Wacom Tablet: I don’t know how I edited without this. An absolute must have to save wrists and sanity.

  1. Solly Baby Wraps: I never would have survived toting my 3 month old around for 6 days while at Click Away without my Solly Baby Wrap. I love how lightweight and stretchy the fabric is, and it’s comfortable for both of us. Our Solly is baby’s favorite daytime napping place — perfect for her and for me because we get extra snuggle time!
  2. Smashbox BB Cream: I’ve tried a couple different BB creams and Smashbox remains my favorite. It’s lightweight, stays on, and has just the right amount of coverage.
  3. Edelkrone SliderPlus: I’ve been drooling over this slider for 2 years and I finally decided it was time to treat myself. I’ve only played with it a little bit so far but I’m loving the results I’m getting, the ease of use, and how portable it is.
  4. Feather Baby Clothes: I received four gorgeous pieces of Feather Baby clothing as gifts for my new daughter from my thoughtful co-workers. I love how soft the cotton is, and the colors and styles are so timeless. They are my favorite for baby clothing!

  1. Instant Pot: I cannot live without this thing! It makes quick work of an entire squash, a mess of potatoes, or and kind of bean in MINUTES. I use this all the time and it has made me a happy camper in the kitchen.
  2. Baby Quasar Clear Rayz: My hormonally challenged face loves this thing! My skin is very acne prone, but also super sensitive/reactive. This is a great way to battle spots without causing more trouble.
  3. Nespresso Aeroccino: This gadget is so amazing! It turns Costco soy milk into a thick and frothy whipped ‘cream’. I cannot have dairy and this makes me, and my coffee, so happy.
  4. Garlands of Grace: I love headbands and this company makes one-of-a-kind head bands and hair coverings that are just gorgeous. I get headaches from headbands, but they offer ties that make them super comfortable.
  5. Wholetones: I listen to this series of songs every single night and also have a copy in my camper! It is the best way I know to soothe my nerves, help me sleep, and eliminate stress. They work! It took a couple of listens to get my body used to it, but now my husband wakes up and starts it if I forget! I believe they are truly healing and couldn’t live without them now.

  1. Amazon Echo: We listen to a lot of music in our house, and the Echo has made it so much easier! The voice recognition is excellent, making it very easy to pull up anything from our library and many subscription services. As a bonus, she tells jokes to our kids, helps mange our grocery list, and even places simple amazon orders for us.
  2. Madewell Denim: Finally – jeans that fit me and don’t stretch out! I’ve been frustrated by jeans that fit beautifully in the morning, but are horribly saggy within hours. Madewell jeans actually hold their shape without being too stiff. (Now I just wish they had more styles.)
  3. iBert Safe-T-Seat Front Child Seat: I dream of a cargo bike to move my whole crew, but for now I settle for the iBert. I love this seat for my youngest. To me it’s a lot more stable than the rear-mounted seats, and I can talk to and interact with my little passenger while we ride.
  4. Fujifilm X100T: I made the switch to all mirrorless last year and haven’t looked back. I bought this camera from a CMer this year and it’s the perfect complement to my X-T1. I love how simple and fun it is to shoot with and the image quality consistently blows me away.
  5. Lush Rehab Shampoo: My hair is relatively healthy, so I was skeptical when the Lush salesperson recommended Rehab. She was right I love it. My hair looks good, I can get away with less washing, and my scalp loves it. Not to mention a bottle of this stuff lasts lasts forever.

  1. Roomba: I love this little guy so much! When I asked my husband to buy me this for my birthday he begrudging obliged (you know happy wife, happy life and all!). And now even he’s a fan. My kids actually like to clean their rooms so he can come up and zoom around!
  2. Mini Keurig: I really love how small this brewer is, it barely takes up any space on the counter! I don’t have to worry about the water sitting in the bin, you fill it each time with enough for that cup. This gets a lot of use when you have a newborn at home.
  3. Honest Company: Another post baby must for me. I got my first package of newborn sized diapers before Leighton was born and I haven’t had to go to the store once to buy diapers since! I especially love their wipes, they are so soft and thick. The patterns are super cute too!
  4. Towel Pants: My daughter is a competitive swimmer and we are out at swim practices or meets SIX days a week! She has become obsessed with these pants. She throws them on over her bathing suit at meets or after practice. Sometimes she lounges in them all day on the weekend because she says they are that comfortable.

  1. Sedu Flat Iron: I love how silky my hair gets after I use this. My hair is a little wavy and the Sedu glides through the hair and straighten it with just one pass. I have the 1″ which works great for my hair length.
  2. aden + anais swaddle blankets: These are my favorite swaddle blanket. They are so soft and the size are big enough to swaddle an older baby. I love the Earthly color because they photograph beautifully.
  3. Moby Wrap Baby Carrier: I got this as a gift 4 years ago before my first was born. I tried it a couple of times and hated it. I use it again this time around with my second after watching tutorials on youtube and fell in love with it.
  4. Boscia Purifying Cleansing Gel: I’ve been using this product for a few years now. I don’t wear make-up on a daily basis aside from some moisturizer and compact powder so I don’t need a heavy cleanser. I love that this one is light and sulfate free so it doesn’t dry my skin and the fragrance is not overwhelming.

  1. Osprey Porter Travel Backpack: This travel backpack has changed my life! It’s the size of a normal carry on, in the form of a backpack, with thoughtfully laid out details to help keep me organized. My clothes and personal items go in the main part (that has its own compartments to keep things neat and easy to use/find), my laptop and various cords/chargers go in the padded organizational front pocket, and any liquids to in the top zipper pocked for easy removal when going through security at the airport. I’ve taken this on week-long trips and always end up with more clothing than I used, and I even took this on my 2 week trip to Europe this October. It fits in the overhead bins of the airlines so I never have to worry about my luggage getting lost or anything mysteriously disappearing. I haven’t checked a bag in over a year! There is a hip strap them at helps distribute the weight and makes it easy to carry, and both the hip and shoulder straps can be stowed away to use it as a duffel with only its handles. I have the red one, my husband has the same one in black, and we bought our sons each their own Porter 30 (same design, smaller size) for Christmas this year. Can you tell I love it?
  2. Eagle Creek Packing Cubes – Pack-It Specter Starter Set: I use these to maximize the space in my Porter 46 mentioned above. I especially love the garment folder because it allows me to transport things around the country or world and have them arrive wrinkle-free. I use this one for slacks, dresses and nicer shirts. I use the smaller packing cubes for t-shirts, casual clothing and undergarments. You wouldn’t believe the amount of clothing you can fit into these 3 pieces and then into the Porter 46. They’re also super lightweight, so they don’t add additional heft to your luggage, which is great when you’re carrying it on your back. But these can be used with any style of carry on, from a regular roller carryon to a traditional backpack to maximize the small space and still feel like you have “options” when you travel. Because even though I pack light, I still like to have options.
  3. TSA PreCheck Status: Keeping with the travel theme, I can’t rave enough about TSA PreCheck! I applied in person, it took all of 15 minutes, and the next business day I was given my known traveler number as approval. I save a crazy amount of time not having to go through the long security lines and any children traveling with you (up to age 13, when they need their own) can go through the TSA PreCheck line as well. No more taking off your shoes, pulling out laptops and liquids, taking off cardigans, etc and the line is always SHORT. The $85 fee gets you 5 years of TSA PreCheck status and it was 100% worth it from the first time I flew that way.
  4. Let the Good Times Roll Facial Cleanser: I love this cleanser/exfoliator from Lush Cosmetics! I never knew how much I loved exfoliating until I started using this one. The scent is so amazing, it smells like something you could eat! It is gentle but effective and leaves my face feeling so soft and smooth that I barely need to apply a separate moisturizer afterward. I switch up facial products every so often, but this is one that never goes out of the rotation. Plus, it’s vegan so you can feel good about your purchase.

  1. Athleta Bettona Jegging: I live in these. They feel like leggings but the pockets make me feel better about wearing them almost daily. They look great with shorter tops or tunics!
  2. Goodreads: 2015 was my year to start reading again (a favorite pastime I gave up after kiddos) and this app is super helpful when I’m overwhelmed at the bookstore. While I’m not as great at updating it as I should be, the reviews are fantastic!
  3. Urban Decay B-6 Spray: Every time I run out of this, I convince myself I don’t need it. Then two days later I’m ordering it again. It makes my makeup go on so much smoother, and makes my skin look so much brighter.
  4. Coconut Oil: I use coconut oil for everything- cooking, baking, teeth whitening, shaving cream, moisturizer. There are jars of it all over my house!

  1. Oral Ivy: This may seem like an odd product for my favorite things, but poison ivy has been a problem for me since childhood. Many times I end up with it after photographing my children in field and parks. I have tried so many over the counter products and none have worked until this! Before finding this product my last bout of poison ivy lasted 4 months with several rounds of steroids to get rid of it. My mom told me about this, and that she heard from my uncle that it actually works. I came down with poison ivy while camping this year, and I kid you not the itching stopped shortly after taking this product and it cleared up very quickly!
  2. Krud Kleaner: After reading an article by a cleaning expert about Krud Kleaner I bought it. I love it as it pretty much cleans anything, and cleans it well without any toxic fumes!! I use it in my bathroom, on my cabinets, my siding of my house and so much more!
  3. MeFOTO Roadtrip Travel Tripod: I already had a tripod I love, but it was much too heavy to bring to Click Away so I bought this one based off numerous recommendations from other photographers. It worked so great for traveling and was still very steady and easy to setup!
  4. Dicapac Underwater Camera Case: I love underwater shots, but I don’t have a pool and wasn’t sure I would use underwater housing enough to justify the price. I had previously bought an underwater point and shoot camera and while fun it lacked some of the features I was used to with my dslr camera so I researched to find a different option for this past summer. I ended up buying a dicapac bag for my mirrorless Samsung camera. I was very impressed with how well my shots turned out from this combo and think it’s a great inexpensive alternative to buying underwater housing.

  1. Waste No Time: A browser extension for Safari or Chrome that lets you choose how long you can spend on a given website each day. I have mine set to limit my FB and other social media browsing to 30 minutes during business hours on weekdays. Just knowing that the app is watching me has helped cut down on the time I waste each day. Also? It’s free!
  2. Maid Naturally Cleaning Products: When I lived in Spokane WA a friend of mine started using a cleaning company who exclusively cleaned with their own handmade, natural products. They also sold their products to the public and I quickly fell in love with how well the products worked and with their scents! My absolute favorite is the “Cyprus Spray.” Scented with Clary Sage and Vanilla, I use it to clean wood surfaces, polish door knobs and dust anywhere else I can think to spray it.
  3. Olivine Atelier Perfume: I was recently on the hunt for a new perfume. After running across this perfumery on Etsy I ordered a sampler. While all of her scents are lovely, I just can’t get enough of Full Regalia. It is a beautiful, grown up Vanilla scent- not too sweet or cloying, with a very tiny hint of mellow fruit.
  4. Remy Side Chairs: I’ve been dreaming about a new dining room table and chairs for years. Our previous set was great, but I didn’t love it. We were lucky enough to have a refectory style table handmade out of walnut by a Sicilian craftsman and I had decided that Tolix chairs would be the perfect compliment. Unfortunately, authentic Tolix chairs weren’t in the budget. After a few hours of internet research I found the Remy chairs on Restoration Hardware. I waited a long time for these chairs, and they didn’t disappoint. Solid, sturdy, easy wipe down cleaning and standing up wonderfully to kid abuse… plus they’re cute! What more could I ask for?

  1. Dior AirFlash Foundation: It is pricey, but much less so than time travel…LOL! Seriously, this spray foundation goes on lightly and covers in a way that makes imperfections disappear without being too makeup-y. It is Photoshop in a bottle.
  2. Stitch Fix subscription: If you have that wife/mom/sister/friend who has everything, THIS is what you should gift her this year. A box of five items specifically picked for your body type by a pro stylist arrives at your doorstep as often as you choose. I was a skeptic until I tried it and it is as if everything is tailored just for me!
  3. Stumptown Hairbender Coffee: Once you are introduced to coffee this good, there is no going back. It is sweet and rich and pairs particularly well with a good English toffee.
  4. Click Away 2015 Streaming Videos Bundle: I had such an amazing time in Texas this year and this bundle lets me relive the magic over and over again.
  5. Converse Lo Sneaker: You can’t go wrong with a pair of classic Chucks. I wear these with everything and they always work. So comfy and cute!

  1. Hydro Flask: Fill your hydro flask with ice water in the morning and you will still have ice water in the afternoon without any condensation around the bottle. It can also keep your coffee or hot beverages hot all day long which is important on those busy days.
  2. Pacifica Natural Indian Coconut Nectar Body Butter: This Indian coconut nectar body butter is really special. The scent is light and heady at the same time. There are notes of vanilla as well as coconut and itt goes on smooth and isn’t greasy at all. Vegan.
  3. Laura Zindel Coffee Mug: Vermont artist Laura Zindel’s richly detailed illustrations makes this coffee mug the perfect gift for the coffee connoisseur in your life.
  4. Young Living Essential Oils: Thanks to the combination of Lavender and Peace and Calming, I now sleep soundly through the night…every night.
  5. Kate Spade Watch: This watch is the perfect size and shape, and offers the beautiful details that make it feminine.

  1. DeLallo Dipping Spices: These things are so good! Grab a fresh baguette of french bread and some great olive oil, mix these in and they are SO tasty. For the longest time, I had these on my subscribe and save on Amazon because we went through them so often!
  2. Raising Tiny Humans mug: Mine was a gift from my best friend and not only is the mug just so appropriate- it holds SO.MUCH. coffee (which is all that’s keeping me alive at this point.
  3. doTERRA On Guard Foaming Hand Wash: It smells so good- and while I’m not as “oily” as some- I swear since we started using this as our only handsoap a year ago, we have hardly gotten sick at all!
  4. Furiously Happy: This book (the second by “The Blogess” aka Jenny Lawson) had me laughing out loud so much that my husband was probably thinking I’d lost my mind. If you aren’t already a fan- this book will make you want to sit down and read her blog from the very beginning!

  1. Scuba Hoodie by Lululemon: I can’t get enough of this hoodie (I have 3 in different colours). It’s comfy, warm, well made and did I mention warm? This is perfect for layering under a vest or winter jacket and it’s not so snug that I can’t add extra layers underneath if I need to. Most days this hoodie is all I need, it’s that warm.
  2. T-fal OptiGrill: This little counter-top grill has become our favourite kitchen appliance. It’s versatile, cooks meat perfectly every time, and our teenagers can use it make themselves an after school grilled cheese sandwich.
  3. Hotel Tonight App: This app helps travellers find last minute accommodation. It proved invaluable for us earlier this summer as we decided to take a spontaneous trip into Manhattan with our kids. We needed a hotel room big enough for 4 adult sized bodies, within walking distance to everything we wanted to see. We tried everything and then realized we had the unused Hotel Tonight app on our phones. Within minutes we had a 1 bedroom suite just outside Times Square at a significantly discounted rate. This is my go-to app when I’m looking for hotel rooms.
  4. Hitcase: This stays on my iPhone almost all the time. I love the fact that the case is waterproof, but more importantly, the interchangeable lenses mean I can create a varied number of images with my phone. My youngest son has this case plus the chest strap and mounts his phone to video us doing all sorts of activities like mountain biking and skiing.

  1. Hobo Wallet: Stylishly elegant and functional… this is THE perfect wallet to drop in your purse or to carry on its own. No more fumbling trying not to drop things from my hands… this wallet has enough space inside to hold my iPhone, keys and even my favorite lip gloss while still being sleek enough to get what I need from it in a hurry. Love that it makes a great clutch for dressy occasions too… I actually saw about 5 of them at the ClickAway Voice reception last month.
  2. Market tote: To and from school, appointments, errands and sessions, I can’t live without my market tote. I can drop anything I need for the day and carry it with me in my car. I never have to worry about it tipping over, and it keeps everything I need in an easy to find place. I use this at least 3 times a week. I’m not sure how I managed to keep up with my things without it!
  3. Lip Fusion XL Lip Plumping Therapy: I love the umph this gives my lips. I apply it before bed and then again when I wake up. My lips feel full and silky, and it’s a must-have in the drier months.
  4. DL 1969 Jeans: Hands down my favorite jeans. These jeans will stretch with your movement, but they keep their shape all day long. I never have to worry about them sagging or loosening up. I bought a dark blue shade as my first pair and quickly added a pair in black to my jeans collection. If I could only wear one pair of jeans for the rest of my life, it would be these.

So thats it. Those are the products were loving right now. What items are you loving?

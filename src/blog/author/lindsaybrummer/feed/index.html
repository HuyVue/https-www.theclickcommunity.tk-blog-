My favorite things with Lindsay Brummer!

1. My Nikon D800:  I love this camera and the massive file sizes of the photos.  The low light capability is really a dream and I would be missing out on some awesome moments for my family and clients if I had not gotten this bad boy.

2. Everyday photos of my sweet kids; real ones, not just iPhone snaps.  I have a personal project that I call Beauty in Every Day; it has helped me enjoy the small moments between the big things that seem to just slip away.

3. Kelly Moore Libby:  This bag can haul it all and it’s super cute.  I love the brown leather and the way it snugs up over my shoulder.

4. Brooks Running Shoes:  These shoes are my favorite for logging those long miles on hot Arizona days or cold Idaho mornings.

5. Chatbooks:  I love having my instagram photos printed to Chatbooks and the kids love looking through them.  I am about to order the series for the grandparents also.

6. My BOB stroller:  This guy helps me log all those miles when I can’t get out of bed early enough to go at it alone.  It handles perfectly off road or on the paths in my cute new Idaho neighborhood.

7. The Samsung NX30:  This mirrorless camera can fit in any purse or diaper bag and the images can pop on over to the iPhone nicely.

8. Mac Twig lipstick and ample pink lip gloss:  The perfect color for every occasion.

9. The Lensbaby group became a fast favorite for me, helping to express movement, to change a story and really convey what I see without having to physically remove the clutter from an image.

10. iPhone 6 plus:  I love this monster of a phone and everything that it does.  It has become a constant companion, keeping in touch with all of the sweet friends I miss dearly in Arizona and of course to keep the kids in touch with their grandparents in different states.
